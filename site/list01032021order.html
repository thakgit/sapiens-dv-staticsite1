My CORN Pakoda Recipe :
  1. This recipe is best made with frozen corn. Coarsely grind frozen corn. Make sure it’s not gound to a paste.
  2. Add grated carrots, chopped capsicum, and green chillies. You can add any veggies of your choice. Just make sure the veggies are finely chopped/grated so that they bind together and do not fall apart.
  3. Now ad the seasoning- black pepper powder, salt and chaat masala
  4. Mix them all together and let it rest for 5-7 mins. If the temperature is warm, let it rest for a shorter time.
  5. Resting will allow the corn to defrost a little bit, releasing enough moisture to bind the cutlet together.
  6. Now add the corn flour nd rice flour. Rice flour adds crispinedd to the pakodas . If you don’t have rice flour, you can add maida but it will nnot give the same texture.
  7. Shape the pakodas/cutlets in your desired shape.
  8. Fry on medium heat until golden.
  9. Enjoy with a hot cup of tea or coffee.
